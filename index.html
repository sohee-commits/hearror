music we do

HEARROR is a unique music collective formed in the heart of the digital age. Comprised of a diverse group of artists, the group's members come from various walks of life, each bringing their unique perspective and style to the table.

Their music, a blend of horrorcore and EDM, is a testament to their ability to fuse different genres and create a sound that is both hauntingly familiar and eerily new. Their lyrics, often dark and introspective, delve into the human psyche, exploring themes of love, loss, and the unknown. The group's name, a play on the word "fear," encapsulates their ability to evoke fear and inspire thought through their music.
